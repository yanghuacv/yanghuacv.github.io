Yang Hua (华 扬)

Lecturer (Assistant Professor)

Queen's University Belfast, UK

I will start as a Lecturer (equivalent to Assistant Professor) at Queen’s University Belfast, UK in July 2017 and collaborate closely with Prof. Neil Robertson.

I obtained my Ph.D. under the supervision of Dr. Cordelia Schmid and Dr. Karteek Alahari in Inria LEAR/THOTH team, France. My Ph.D. research was funded by the scholarship of the Microsoft Research - Inria joint center. I obtained my Master’s degree from Peking University Shenzhen Graduate School, China, advised by Prof. Yuexian Zou.

I took charge of person re-identification project as a research scientist in AnyVision from 2016 to 2017. I engaged in a long-term research project and the related industrial product development as a senior R&D engineer in Panasonic R&D Center Singapore from 2008 to 2012.

My interests focus on fundamental research topics and real-world applications in computer vision based on a variety of machine learning approaches including kernel methods, energy minimization, probabilistic graphical models and deep learning. Currently, I work on

  • Visual object recognition
  • Visual object tracking
  • Person re-identification and face recognition for real-world scenarios
  • Video understanding in context of RGB/infrared data
